How It Works

Three steps to effortless qualification

A seamless process designed around how UK letting agents actually work.

01

Applicant submits enquiry

Share your unique RentSift link on your Rightmove profile or property listings. Applicants complete a structured screening form in under two minutes.

02

Our AI checks income against UK affordability thresholds, reviews document readiness, identifies student applicants, detects urgency, and validates postcodes — giving you an instant score.

Features

Designed for UK lettings workflows

Every feature built around UK letting standards, agent workflows, and real tenant behaviour.

Document Readiness Check

Every applicant assessed for document readiness as part of the UK letting process. Flags incomplete or uncertain cases before they reach your diary.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 June 2025 · Service provided for the UK market

1. Who We Are

RentSift ("we", "us", "our") operates rentsift.co.uk and provides AI-powered rental applicant screening software for UK letting agents. For any privacy-related enquiries, please contact us at hello@rentsift.co.uk.

2. Data We Collect

  • Personal details of subscribing agents: name, email address, telephone number
  • Rental applicant data submitted via our screening system: name, income, employment status, postcode, document status, and message content
  • Payment information, processed securely by our payment provider — we do not store card details
  • Technical usage data and cookies for service improvement and analytics

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the RentSift screening service
  • To process subscription payments and manage your account
  • To send service-related notifications and product updates
  • To assist agents in organising and prioritising applicant data

4. Legal Basis (UK GDPR)

We process personal data under the following legal bases: performance of a contract (to deliver our service), legitimate interests (service improvement and security), and legal obligation (UK regulatory compliance).

5. Data Retention

Agent account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus six months thereafter. Rental applicant data is automatically deleted after 30 days for unsuitable applicants and after 12 months for all other records.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, or port your personal data. Contact us at hello@rentsift.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using AES-256 encryption. We use Supabase as our primary data processor, hosted on servers located in the United Kingdom (London region).

8. Contact

For all privacy-related queries: hello@rentsift.co.uk
