How It Works

Three steps to effortless qualification

A seamless process designed around how UK letting agents actually work.

01

Applicant submits enquiry

Share your unique RentSift link on your Rightmove profile or property listings. Applicants complete a structured qualification form in under two minutes.

02

AI qualifies in seconds

Our AI verifies income against the UK 30× rule, assesses right to rent eligibility, identifies student applicants, detects urgency, and validates postcodes.

03

You meet serious tenants only

Receive an instant notification with a complete applicant profile and qualification score. Every minute of your time goes to genuinely promising prospects.

Features

Designed for UK lettings compliance

Every feature built around UK law, agent workflows, and real tenant behaviour.

⚖️

Right to Rent Screening

Every applicant assessed per the Immigration Act 2014. Automatic flagging of incomplete or uncertain cases before they reach your diary.

💷

Income Verification

Checks annual income against the UK standard 30× monthly rent rule. Guarantor requests triggered automatically when income falls short.

🎓

Student Detection

Identifies student applicants and reassesses qualification criteria around upfront payment capability and UK-based guarantor availability.

📍

Postcode Validation

Validates UK postcodes and flags applicants located outside your target area — preventing wasted viewings before they are booked.

⚡

Urgency Detection

Identifies applicants with immediate move requirements and elevates their qualification score, ensuring your best prospects are never missed.

📊

Live Dashboard

A single, clean view of every applicant: qualification scores, AI analysis notes, and status filters — Qualified, Pending, or Rejected.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 January 2025 · Governed by the laws of England and Wales

1. Who We Are

RentSift ("we", "us", "our") operates rentsift.co.uk and provides AI-powered rental applicant qualification software for UK letting agents. For any privacy-related enquiries, please contact us at hello@rentsift.co.uk.

2. Data We Collect

  • Personal details of subscribing agents: name, email address, telephone number
  • Rental applicant data submitted via our qualification system: name, income, employment status, postcode, right to rent status, and message content
  • Payment information, processed securely by LemonSqueezy — we do not store card details
  • Technical usage data and cookies for service improvement and analytics

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the RentSift qualification service
  • To process subscription payments and manage your account
  • To send service-related notifications and product updates
  • To assist with compliance obligations including Right to Rent checks under the Immigration Act 2014

4. Legal Basis (UK GDPR)

We process personal data under the following legal bases: performance of a contract (to deliver our service), legitimate interests (service improvement and security), and legal obligation (UK regulatory compliance).

5. Data Retention

Agent account data is retained for the duration of your subscription plus six months thereafter. Rental applicant data is retained for twelve months, in line with UK lettings industry standards and regulatory guidance.

6. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, or port your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at hello@rentsift.co.uk. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit using TLS and at rest using AES-256 encryption. We use Supabase and Make.com as data processors; both maintain full GDPR compliance and ISO 27001 certification.

8. Contact

For all privacy-related queries: hello@rentsift.co.uk
