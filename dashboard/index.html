How it works

When a prospective tenant sends an enquiry through Rightmove, Zoopla, or OnTheMarket, RentSift receives it, extracts the applicant's details, and runs an AI screening check using UK letting standards — income thresholds, document readiness, employment type, and urgency signals. You receive a scored result instantly.
